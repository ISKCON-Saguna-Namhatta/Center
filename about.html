    Our Mission

    At Saguna Namhatta (ISKCON), we strive to create a welcoming environment that encourages spiritual development and self-realization. Our mission is to spread the teachings of Bhagavad Gita, promote vegetarianism, and help individuals experience the transformative power of Krishna consciousness. We aim to build a vibrant, inclusive community where everyone can grow spiritually and contribute to the well-being of the world.

    Our Activities

    • Bhagavad Gita Classes: Join our weekly classes to learn the profound and timeless teachings of the Bhagavad Gita. These sessions are designed to deepen your understanding of life's purpose, human nature, and the path to spiritual awakening.
    • Festivals: Participate in grand celebrations such as Janmashtami, Rath Yatra, and other special occasions. These festivals not only offer a chance to celebrate but also help build a strong sense of community and devotion among devotees.
    • Food Distribution: Join our efforts in providing nutritious vegetarian meals to those in need. Our food distribution programs aim to serve the community while promoting the benefits of a vegetarian lifestyle as part of our spiritual practice.

    Our Leadership

    Saguna Namhatta (ISKCON) is guided by our respected President, who provides spiritual leadership and ensures the temple's activities align with the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita. Under the leadership of our president, the community thrives and grows, fostering an atmosphere of devotion, unity, and service. The leadership team is dedicated to nurturing the spiritual lives of all devotees and guiding them on the path of Krishna consciousness.
