Rath Yatra Festival 2025

Date: 27th June 2025 (Ashadha Shukla Dwitiya)

Time: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Location: Jagannath Temple, Puri

The Sacred Journey of Lord Jagannath

For the past two years, the Sri Chaitanya Chandra Mandir has been a beacon of spiritual enlightenment and divine festivities. Among its most significant celebrations is the Rath Yatra, a grand festival that draws devotees from far and wide to witness the majestic procession of Lord Jagannath, Baladev, and Subhadra. This year, the temple is set to celebrate the Rath Yatra on an even grander scale, filling the hearts of devotees with joy and devotion.

Rath Yatra, also known as the Chariot Festival, is one of the most revered events in Hindu tradition. It symbolizes Lord Jagannath’s journey from His temple to the Gundicha Temple, allowing devotees who cannot enter the sanctum to receive His divine blessings. The festival represents the Lord’s desire to come out and bless His devotees, breaking all barriers of caste, creed, and religion.
